What does this do?

GrammarParrot is an assistive website that converts your words that are spoken aloud to electronic written texts. Then, it notes for any grammatical error you might have made in your speech to review and improve!

Why GrammarParrot?

When you speak with proper grammar, not only will others be interested in and able to understand what you have to say, but they also will be much more likely to see you as intelligent, logical, and educated. On the other hand, speaking in poor grammar may leave others to judge you negatively or fail to take you seriously. This may earn you a less than desirable grade in your education, but it can also affect your job opportunities. Hiring managers across the country cite poor grammar as a reason for not considering a prospective job applicant.

GrammarParrot™ will help to:

  • Speak English fluently and confidently
  • Be more clear with the meaning and clarity of your intended message
  • Develop a habit of thinking logically and coherently
  • Build groundwork for effective communication

Who should use this?

As GrammarParrot™ is accessible to everyone around the world, anyone is welcomed to try it out. Language is involved with almost everything we do as human beings and there are no limits to learning at any age. GrammarParrot™ is easy-to-use for all ages, press the record button and speak away!

This website is best for for international students, immigrants and English Secondary Learners who are working on their English speaking. If they are able to repeatedly practice speaking with proper grammar, they will not feel exclued anymore, but more confident in expressing themselves. Eventually, it would help to overcome theirlanguage barriers.

Furthermore, GrammarParrot™ can help you prepare for job interviews, school presentations, communicating at work, networking and with even leaving great first impressions.
